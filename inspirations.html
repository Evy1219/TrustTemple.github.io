My Dad

The person who is truly subconsciously shaping me as a man and father is my own father. If it weren't for him, I wouldn't have as much knowledge about basic life necessities and responsibilities. He also was the person to introduce me to the computer world and everything I do from a singular keystroke gives me a reminder of where and who helped me get where I am. Thank you Dad. I love you.
